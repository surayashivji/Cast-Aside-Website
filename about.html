what's up?
There are conversations to be had in the Entertainment Industry. From diversity to unions to stereotypes to award shows, we have our work cut out for us! Are you ready to listen to the perspective of a child actor, screenwriter, and diverse teenager — all in one podcast? Assuming the answer is yes...
hey! i’m jameel shivji.
After spending the first 12 years of my life in the Silicon Valley, where my only exposure to the Entertainment Industry was the hours I spent watching Dan Schneider shows, I moved to LA and started working as a professional actor. My raw passions drove me to Hollywood, and my experiences here have driven me to create this podcast, where I give every issue a voice.
