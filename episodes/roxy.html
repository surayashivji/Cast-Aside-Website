Roxy Cowan
February 17th 2019
Roxy Cowan is a 17 year old filmmaker. She’s a full time high school student, but managed to write, direct, produce, and edit a feature film over the past year. Roxy’s father, Rob Cowan, brought box office hits such as Aquaman, The Conjuring, and a multitude of Mellissa Mccarthy films to the big screen. She’s incredibly excited to attend Chapman Film School in the Fall!
In this episode, Roxy discusses gender equality, growing up in the Entertainment Industry, and her experiences as a young woman on set.
