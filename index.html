latest episode
March 4th 2019
Madison De La Garza is a screenwriter and actress who has been working in the industry since age 7. She is best known for her role as "Juanita Solis" on Desperate Housewives. Most recently, Madison wrote and starred in a short film called Subject 16. In this episode, Madison discusses her take on Netflix’s 13 Reasons Why, her experiences with social media, her time as an actress on Desperate Housewives, and her childhood, where she grew up in the spotlight alongside her sisters Dallas and Demi Lovato.
